Task 1

I first considered all of the memorable parts of DMIS that I have done so far. Then I removed the ones I thought would be too broad for this task or that wouldn't be as engaging to a wider audience. Next I brainstormed more specific ideas for the remaining broad areas.

  • Data Analysis
    • "The importance of Data Analysis in Game Design"
  • Document Encoding
  • AI
    • "AI and the Decline of Creativity"
    • "AI and it's Danger to Humanity"
  • ECCO
  • 3D Modelling
    • "How to Preserve the Past with 3D Modelling and Why it's Important"
  • Digitisation
  • Ethics
    • "How Digital Media changed our View on Ethics"
  • Digital Media in Cultural Heritage
  • Databases

It became clear to me while working on this list that AI would be the most engaging and fun topic to talk about. Between the two topics in this area I decided to go with "AI and the Decline of Creativty" as this was a little more grounded and relevant to today. I also decided to reword it to "Is AI Destroying Creativity? And should we be Worried?" to frame it more as two seperate questions which should allow for more intrigue and back-and-fourth.

Task 2

Some questions I want to answer in my website include:

I think the following groups would or could be interested in my website:

  • Artists
  • Other Creative People
  • People who Train AI
  • Investors in AI
  • People who Create AI Art
  • Average people who are interested in AI and Art
